About Me

I am a versatile freelance graphic designer passionate about visual storytelling. Whether it's crafting memorable logos, designing striking posters, or building cohesive branding materials and web visuals, I adapt my style to fit each client's unique vision.

From minimal and professional to bold, modern, and playful aesthetics, my focus is always on clean layouts, strong typography, and balanced colors.

I can work from existing client ideas, reference material, or completely from scratch. Reliable, detail-oriented, and open to feedback, I'm dedicated to delivering high-quality designs that exceed expectations.

Common Questions

How many revisions do I get?

I typically offer 2 to 3 rounds of revisions for every project. My goal is to make sure you are 100% happy with the final design. Any major changes outside the original project scope may incur a small extra fee.

What are your payment terms?

For most projects, I require a 50% upfront deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon final approval before the raw high-resolution files are handed over.

How long does a logo design usually take?

A completely custom logo design typically takes between 3 to 7 days, depending on the complexity and how quickly we communicate during the revision phases.

Will I get the source files?

Yes! Once the final payment is cleared, you will receive all relevant high-resolution exports (PNG, JPG, PDF) as well as the original vector or source files (AI, PSD) depending on your package.
